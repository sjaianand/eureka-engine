About Jai Sekar

Are you feeling accomplished but unfulfilled? Successful on paper, yet stuck in a rut? You’ve checked every box and done everything “right.” But does it feel like something is missing—in your work or life?

I’ve been there. My own search for clarity began years ago when I sought a coach to navigate my own high-stakes career. I walked away from that experience knowing something vital was missing: a bridge between hard-edged business strategy and the human being behind the corporate title.

For nearly 25 years, I’ve led teams and shaped strategy across continents—from India, France, and the UK to the USA. My background spans large-scale technology delivery leadership at Capgemini, pure-play management consulting at Booz & Company (now Strategy&), and driving greenfield business segments at the scale and velocity of Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Today, I partner with a select group of global clients in an elite coaching capacity. My strength lies in this dual perspective. I have been in the arena for decades building solutions and growing multi-million dollar businesses in high uncertainty, while bringing the formal credentials and structured support of the International Coaching Federation (ICF).
